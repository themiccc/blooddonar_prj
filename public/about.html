About LifeLine

A mission to reduce blood search times and make voluntary donation seamless for everyone.

Project Objectives

  • ✓ Maintain an active, verified database of voluntary blood donors.
  • ✓ Help patients quickly locate matching blood donors in their immediate vicinity.
  • ✓ Minimise critical time required to search for blood during emergencies.
  • ✓ Provide a premium online administrative dashboard for donor management.
  • ✓ Foster awareness of voluntary blood donation in community healthcare ecosystems.

Project Scope

The Blood Donor Finder System is a premium web application designed to bridges communication gaps between donors and recipients. Users can register as donors, specify their locations, and declare availability. Recipients can filter donors by blood group and location, and directly submit urgent patient blood requests.

Through modular design, secure sessions, and instantaneous queries, LifeLine ensures that critical care patient requests reach matching donors without administrative friction.
